# Inkgredients

**Cook more. Stress less. One recipe a day, made for you.**

---

Deciding what to cook every day is exhausting. Inkgredients fixes that. Open the app, and your recipe is already waiting — perfectly matched to your diet and allergies. No scrolling, no searching, no compromises.

## Features

**Your recipe, your rules**
Set your dietary preferences once — vegetarian, vegan, keto, gluten-free, halal, and more — and every recipe you receive will fit your lifestyle. Allergens like nuts, dairy, shellfish, and gluten are filtered out automatically.

**Shopping list, done for you**
Every recipe comes with a ready-made shopping list. Adjust servings from 1 to 20 and the quantities update instantly. Check off items as you shop.

**Save your favourites**
Found a recipe you love? Tap the heart to save it. Your favourites are always a tap away, ready to cook again.

**Gentle daily reminders**
Inkgredients can remind you when to shop and when to start cooking — so dinner actually happens.

**Not feeling it today?**
No problem. Hit Skip and get a different suggestion.

---

## Who it's for

Anyone who wants to eat well at home but gets stuck on the "what do we make tonight?" question. Whether you're cooking for one or feeding a family, juggling food allergies or following a specific diet — Inkgredients takes care of the planning so you can focus on the cooking.

---

## Subscription

Inkgredients is free to try with a 3-day trial. After that, a simple yearly subscription keeps the recipes coming. Cancel anytime before the trial ends — no charge.
